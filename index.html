Welcome to MoodRay

MoodRay is a smart lamp that uses light and music to improve your emotional well-being. Our product detects your mood and responds with personalized light colors and music to help you feel your best.

About MoodRay

MoodRay was created to support mental health in a fun and interactive way. Whether you're feeling blue or full of energy, MoodRay adjusts its light and sound to match or uplift your mood.

Features

  • Mood detection through voice input and facial expression
  • Dynamic lighting that changes based on your mood
  • Music recommendations through Spotify integration
  • Mood calendar to track your emotional trends
  • Smart home integration
