About MoodRay

MoodRay is a smart mood lamp that blends color, light, and music to reflect and enhance your emotions. Designed for young people to better understand and manage how they feel every day.

Features

  • Color-based mood tracking
  • Spotify music integration
  • Voice input mood detection
  • Smart home compatibility

Sustainability

MoodRay is built with eco-friendly materials and energy-efficient lighting to reduce environmental impact while promoting emotional well-being.
