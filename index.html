Why MoodRay?

MoodRay is a smart, compact, and AI-powered lamp designed to enhance your emotional well-being using synchronized music and lighting. Whether you're feeling stressed or just want to set the vibe, MoodRay responds to your mood to help improve it.

Sleep & Wake Routines

Gentle music and lights to help you wake up or wind down naturally.

How It Works

  1. Tell MoodRay your current mood through the app, touch, or voice.
  2. MoodRay selects music and lighting colors tailored to elevate your mood.
  3. Sit back as the light and music evolve to gradually improve your mental state.
